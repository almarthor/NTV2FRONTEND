About me

Ryan Rodney Reynolds OBC (born October 23, 1976) is a Canadian and American actor, producer and businessman. He began his career starring in the Canadian teen soap opera Hillside (1991–1993), and had minor roles before landing the lead role on the sitcom Two Guys and a Girl between 1998 and 2001. Reynolds then starred in a range of films, including the action film Green Lantern (2011).In January 2018, Reynolds started the production company Maximum Effort and signed a three-year first-look deal with Fox.
